ABOUT  OUR  PROJECT

A Phyics Virtual Lab created with a team of 4 enthusiastic students of PCET'S PIMPRI CHINCHWAD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (ENTC Deptpartment) of First Year , with a view of providing better understanding of physics experiments and also to consolidate skills in design and development of the website.

This Project was done for MPBI session which was introduced to us in Semester 2 of our First Year of Enginering. As we all were passionate about the subject Physics we choose this subject as our Project Domain and integrated it with our  Website development knowlwdge.

We divided the Project Work into 4 members . Ryan Rego  did the core building of the website; using HTML and  CSS languages for the development and designing of the website. Atharv Pachankar did use of Python and little bit of Javascript programming language in the  Website whereas Tanmay Rajput And Vishwasjeet Shirse did the content writing and Background images editing for the website.

Our Mentor  Prof. Anjali Sakurkar Ma'am (Physics Faculty of PCCOE) offered her utmost assistance during the course of making of  this project. She always gave her inputs and guided through each and every step of the project.
